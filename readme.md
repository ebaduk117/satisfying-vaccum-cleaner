#Vacuum It Clean Game
#Created by @ebaduk117

Vacuum It Clean is a little browser game where you drive a vacuum around with WASD and suck up a messy floor before... well, there's no timer, you just gotta get it all clean lol.

##Features

* Customizable vacuum color
* Customizable floor color
* Adjustable mess level (light, medium, heavy)
* Points for every bit of dirt you clean up
* Tough stains that take extra sucking to get rid of (worth more points tho)
* End screen that rates how you did

How to Play

1. Open the game.
2. Pick your vacuum color, floor color, and mess level.
3. Click "make it dirty."
4. Use WASD to drive the vacuum around and sit on the dirt to suck it up.
5. Watch out for the darker tough stains, those take longer.
6. Clean the whole floor and see your rating, then try to beat your score.
